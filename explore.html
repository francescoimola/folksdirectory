Looking for more?

Folks Directory is a community resource built for and by the people of Folkestone. We started in 2024 as a non-profit initiative. We are a small team of volunteers and we fund this resource ourselves.

As we are still growing, there are lots of opportunities for improvement, growth and partnership. We are excited to continue this journey and are moving at the speed of trust. We'd love to hear from you.
